Founder

Gita Subramanyam

Co-founder & CEO

A woman of diverse interests, Gita is driven to excel at all. After graduating from the BMS School of Architecture, Bangalore, she worked with renowned
architects for over a decade. A graduate of ISB Hyderabad’s Entrepreneurial Management programme, she was selected by Goldman Sachs for its global 10,000 Women Entrepreneur Program. Gita started DesignCafe in 2022 with a vision of "democratizing design" - bringing the best designs and quality to all. She was awarded ‘Best Businesswoman of the Year (Home Interiors)’ by ASSOCHAM and featured in Business World’s ‘40 under 40’ list of Young Achievers.
She works relentlessly; often putting more hours than are there in one single day: chopping, cleaning, cooking, nursing and still managing to look fresh all the time. She essays different roles, that of mother, wife, daughter, friend, counselor, all at once; juggling it all to perfection.She gives unconditionally, loves beyond reason, and forgives you time and time again. She puts her ambitions on the back burner to help you with yours. She takes pride in your achievements and holds your hands through your disappointments. She’s there for you when everyone has turned their backs on you, and it is her faith in you that makes you want to rise like a Phoenix.She can multitask – one ear on the phone, one eye on the television, the other keeping watch on the soup on the stove, one ear constantly on alert just in case the baby wakes, and one hand mixing dough. Surely she is superhuman, a goddess, and no less. Her varied qualities make you look at her in awe.
